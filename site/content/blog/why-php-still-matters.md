PHP has the best deploy story of any web language: copy a folder, request a URL. No build, no daemon, no orchestration. Boring is a feature.

The language has also moved on. Strict types, attributes, readonly classes, enums — modern PHP looks closer to TypeScript than to the PHP of 2010. The community just stopped advertising.
