CSS frameworks shipped for the era when CSS was missing variables, nesting, and container queries. All three landed natively. The framework is now competing with the browser.

I still reach for design tokens and a small utility layer. But the fifty-thousand-line stylesheet that promised to solve every layout? I have not opened one in eighteen months.
